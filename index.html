Our Mission

Founded in 1999 by Mahesh Chand, C# Corner started as an online community for software developers and has organically grown from a few hundred members to 3 million registered IT professionals globally with nearly 30 million annual unique visitors. In early 2023, we began monetizing the platform with advertising and will report an estimated $750K in revenue for the fiscal year.

Our mission is to build a knowledge-sharing community where software professionals, companies, and students come together to enrich their understanding and foster innovation.

Through our platform, we offer millions of articles, tutorials, blogs, discussion forums, videos, and news to help developers stay at the forefront of technology.

Our Vision

At C# Corner, we envision a world where developers and IT professionals can seamlessly connect, learn, and grow. Our platform is designed to empower individuals and organizations to achieve their full potential in the tech industry.

Our vision is to create a world where technology is accessible to everyone, and knowledge is shared freely. We aim to empower developers and IT professionals to connect, learn, and grow together, fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation.

Join us in our mission to create a thriving global community of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and leaders.
